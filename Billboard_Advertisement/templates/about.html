About The Project


This is a billboard advertisement system of Bangladesh.

Our mission is to provide an efficient system where dealers and customers can make deals very easily.







The billboard advertisement system of our country has been done manually. Sometimes it is difficult for advertisers to find a proper customer for their

advertisements. Customers also find it difficult to find billboards for their advertisements. By using this application, they can find all the services in one

place. This application can turn the whole countryâ€™s billboard advertisement system into a well-planned system. City corporations can monitor all the

activities. Everyone can view which billboards are currently empty and which billboards are sold to a customer and the deal duration also.
